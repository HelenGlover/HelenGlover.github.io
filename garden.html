Digital Garden


Ideas, notes, and half-baked explorations I'm always tending to. Inspired by the concept of a digital garden, this space offers a more informal and exploratory outlet for my thoughts.

The goal? To cultivate a landscape of information that evolves over time into a richly linked landscape where everything is arranged and connected in ways that allow you to explore - sort of like a real garden. It is not intended to be as well polished as the other pages.

I believe in Learning in Public; the practice of sharing what you learn as you're learning it.
