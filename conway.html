UN SDGS @ Smith

Strengthening the Sustainable Development Goals at Smith College

Timeline: 2023 - 2024
Role: Team Lead

Overview

The UN SDGS at Smith works to promote awareness of the Sustainable Development Goals in three areas: students, academics, and community. Through interdisciplinary learning and integrative collaboration, the team connects practical solution ideas to SDGs challenges throughout the community.

I proposed and currently lead the team. Affiliated with the Conway Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship

Innovation holds immense potential, but without a clear long-term vision, ideas are prone to failure. As a new team, we needed to consider the sustainability of our work which included gaining a deep understanding of our target audience - the Smith community - so we could collaborate with the college’s integrative learning centers to co-create initiatives that address the needs.

In 2024, our team and the Conway Center launched the inaugural Co-Innovate for Joy Challenge. The Challenge invites student teams to create an innovative project/solution that usher in joy while contributing to the advancement of the SDGs.
Whether it’s enhancing well-being, or championing environmental stewardship, ultimately, it is about the recognition of one’s journey of discovery through the experience.
  • $50,000 award pool
  • Annual initiative
  • Aligned with the Hatchery, an idea incubation space for student entrepreneurs led by the entrepreneur-in-residence.
