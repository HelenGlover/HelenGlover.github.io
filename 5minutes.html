Animating Questions

Questions I center what I do around:

  • How can we co-create a society that is ecologically sound, socially just, and economically viable?"
  • How can we use sustainability as an integrative approach to addressing technological and human needs?
  • How can we apply the sustainability elements of resto ration, regeneration, and resilience elements to the world of technology?

More about me

  • I mostly talk about technology or sustainability, but I also love learning psychology and cognitive science. My work is influenced by the teachings of practitioners in these fields.
  • My family and I run an organic family farm for young adults with disabilities. We were inspired by my older brother, who has Down syndrome and autism, to create a community where youth could learn about farming and the environment. Our farm has organic gardens, free-range animals, and lots of chickens.
  • I like to read the terms of conditions for fun. This site Terms of Service; Didn't Read is excellent for ToS summaries on the most common websites
  • I was considering going to undergraduate art school to major in illustration. I eventually decided on Smith College, but I still keep up my art interests through a new form of art: design.
  • I am an early morning riser (around 4 to 5 a.m.). A habit from farm life, I use mornings to prioritize my personal and professional growth goals.
