Precious Plastics

Creative plastic upcycling through design


What if recycling plastics went beyond to changing it into something new?

In a world inundated by environmental disasters, what if our conversations about plastic was centered around joy? In Spring 2021, students supported by the Smith College Design Thinking Initiative and the Center for Design and Fabrication were inspired to create a alternative plastic recycling system in Massachusetts’ Pioneer Valley. The result: the CT River Valley Precious Plastics, a collective group of design labs and colleges transforming local discussions on plastic.
