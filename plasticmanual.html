Precious Plastic Manual

Designing and conducting research for a Precious Plastic user manual

Timeline: 2024
Role: Designer

Overview

CT Precious Plastics is a collective network of local makerspaces and colleges passionate about creatively repurposing plastic waste. Created in Fall 2020 by a student-led team at Smith College, CT Precious Plastics operates two open-sourced machines, hosts workshop events, and creates educational resources.

Opening up knowledge for free is the fastest way to build effective solutions. Bottom-up, grassroots, and community-driven. As part of our design philosophy to prioritize accessible education, one of my projects with Precious Plasitcs is to create a user manual. In ensuring the usability of the manual it was important to consider good information design and clarity in presentation.


The shredder is a machine designed to shred recyclable plastics into smaller flakes. Plastic is chopped up into small, uniform pieces to facilitate consistent melting and easier storage.

The extruder takes the shredded plastic, heats it, and extrudes it through a nozzle as 3D filament. This filament can nicely blend different colors, producing a homogenous and clean color.
