Africa Innovation Challenge

Design and outreach for the Africa Innovation Challenge

Timeline: 2024
Location: Africa
Role: Designer

Overview

In 2024, A Global Innovation Lab for Equality (aGILE) and Oxfam in Africa (OiA) launched the Africa Innovation Challenge, inviting Oxfam teams and partners across Africa to submit proposals for innovative solutions focused on improving civic space or climate-resilient agriculture.

I supported:

  • Cross-functional project management and communication between Agile and OiA
  • Coordination with Country Directors to promote the Challenge within their regions
  • Creation of application form and defining selection criteria for team submissions
  • Operations for information sessions

Design of communication materials

Logo | Official Announcement | Media Graphics | Pamphlet | PowerPoints | Newsletter
