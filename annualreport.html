Annual Report

An online publication that uses visual storytelling to tell the story of aGILE

Timeline: 2024
Role: Designer

Overview

A Global Innovation Lab for Equality (aGILE) is a lab and platform scaling innovative solutions for local and global inequality problems. A comprehensive overview of aGILE and its mission, this report has concise storytelling and eye-catching design to inspire our targeted audience: you.

The main goal was to be strategic in the messaging and layout design. Photographs to break up the text, graphics, and an easy-to-follow narrative for each of aGILE projects.

Setting the mood with aGILE brand styles. The colors combine warmth with vibrant energy, eliciting a sense of inviting harmony. for co-creating solutions.

Simplity is key. The report is the result of multiple iterations to get the copy down to its essential core.

Storytelling is essential in the nonprofit world for connecting supporters to the cause. We emphasize stories and testimonials from our people and participants.
